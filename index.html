How We Work

Four stages, no surprises.

Every engagement follows the same disciplined sequence, regardless of scope.

Diagnose 2 weeks

Two weeks inside your data and your team to find the real constraint — not the one in the org chart. We interview across functions, audit your metrics stack, and identify the true bottleneck that's quietly capping growth.

Design 1 week

A small set of viable paths forward, each pressure-tested against your actual constraints and culture. We present options, not directives — and sequence them so the first win comes within 30 days.

Deploy 4–12 weeks

We build the plan into your existing workflow rather than handing over a binder to interpret alone. Weekly cycles, clear owners, and a live dashboard so every stakeholder sees progress in real time.

Prove Ongoing

We report against the metric we agreed on day one — and stay until it moves. When we leave, you get the playbook, the dashboard, and the confidence to run the system yourself. We're a text away if you need us.
