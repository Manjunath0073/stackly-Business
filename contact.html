Questions

Common enquiries.

Quick answers to the things most people ask before reaching out. If yours isn't here, the form is just a scroll away.

Every engagement is scoped and priced individually based on the outcome you need. Discovery sprints start at ₹2.5L, full engagements typically range from ₹8L–₹25L. We publish our pricing philosophy on our services page — no hidden fees, no hourly creep.

Most engagements run 6–12 weeks from kickoff to handoff. Strategy sprints can be as short as 2 weeks, while embedded advisory relationships often extend to a full quarter or more. We size the timeline to the outcome, not the other way around.

We focus on companies that have found product-market fit and are scaling past 20 people — typically between Series A and Series C. For earlier-stage founders, we offer free monthly office hours. Check our community page for details.

A partner will review your message within 48 hours. If it looks like a good fit, we'll schedule a 30-minute call — no deck, no pitch. Just a direct conversation about where you're stuck and whether we can help. If it's not a fit, we'll say so honestly and point you to resources that might be useful.
