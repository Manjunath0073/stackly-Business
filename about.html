Our Story

Frustration-born: the only consulting firm that made a real difference.

Stackly started the way most good ideas do — inside a bad one. Our founders met at a large consultancy where success was measured in billable hours, not business outcomes. Projects ran long, recommendations piled up, and the team that diagnosed the problem was never the same team that was supposed to fix it.

We left to build something that worked differently. A firm where engagements are scoped around a specific outcome, priced before the work starts, and led by someone who has actually done the job before. No junior analysts. No scope creep. No disappearing after the presentation.

Since 2020, we've run over 120 engagements across six sectors — and nearly every one has come through referral or repeat business. We intend to keep it that way.

Today, Stackly works with companies at every stage of growth: from seed-stage startups building their first operating system, to enterprise businesses navigating transformation. Every engagement shares the same DNA — a defined outcome, a senior team, fixed pricing, and a commitment to stay until the metric moves.

Our DNA

Four beliefs that shape every engagement.

These aren't wall-art values. They're operating principles that we publish to our team page so clients can hold us to them.

01

Senior talent is the only talent

We don't staff with junior analysts or freshly-minted MBAs. Every person on an engagement has held the title they're advising on.

02

Scope before price, outcome before scope

Every engagement starts with a single question: "What needs to be different when this is done?" Then we scope backward from there.

03

Accountability is a feature, not a promise

We define the success metric on day one, report against it publicly inside your team, and adjust course when the data says to.

04

Small teams, deep trust

We deliberately stay small enough that every client works directly with a principal. No account managers. No handoffs.
